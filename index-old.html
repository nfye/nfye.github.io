ABOUT

NFYE is a collection for Sneakerheads and NFT crypto lovers combined. It is an exclusive set of 20,000 collectibles with a purpose to bridge real life sneakers and NFTs together. Together we can introduce NFTs to sneaker culture worldwide and create a foundation that can lead to integration of real world fashion as a whole.

TOTAL MINTED

0/20,000

Minting is the process of buying an NFT at retail price. You will need to connect your MetaMask wallet on the day of the release and will need to hold Ethereum in your MetaMask wallet to purchase one of our exclusive NFYE NFTs.

ROADMAP

The NFYE NFT Collection is a digitally verifiable, tradable, and fixed supply of digital assets created as an exclusive collaboration and is the intersection of the sneakerhead and NFT Community.

The NFYE community is inclusive, however NFYE holders will have exclusive access to the following perks within the NFYE Community:
• Monthly NFYE Community Giveaways
• Digital Drops: NFTs of hyped pairs, airdropped to NFYE holders wallets
• Limited Edition NFYE Merch Drops
• Brand Collaborations, both Merch and NFT Drops
• Artist Collaborations, both Merch and NFT Drops
• AMAs with Artists working in collaboration with the NFYE Community

Our Goal is to give NFYE holders and the community both physical and digital rewards, and create an ecosystem that has real utility and rich incentives.

PHASE 1

Commence Monthly NFYE Community Giveaways

First Digital Drop:

• We will airdrop a Digital pair of hyped sneakers to our NFYE holders. The Community will hold a vote on which pair and the most hyped pair will be dropped.

PHASE 2

First NFYE Digital Drop: Our first NFYE NFT airdrop.

• Each wallet containing an NFYE NFT will be airdropped 1 NFT from the Collection.

PHASE 3

NFYE VR

• Enable holders to combine their Digital Drop + NFYE Digital Drop to generate their NFYE VR

• Goal: Port NFYE VRs into the Sandbox Metaverse as useable Avatars

• NFYE VR owners will be able to pair their NFT to their favorite pair of sneakers

• When the sneakers are viewed through a VR Lens, the NFYE will populate the pair of sneakers.

PHASE 4

• Announce first Artist Collaboration

• NFYE VR holders will have exclusive access to one-time Merch and NFT drops, created in collaboration with the Artist

• Announce first Brand Collaboration

• NFYE VR holders will have exclusive access to one-time Merch and NFT drops, created in collaboration with the Brand.
