ABOUT

NFYE is a collection for Sneakerheads and NFT crypto lovers combined. It is a Exclusive set of 20,000 collectibles with a purpose to bridge real life sneakers and NFTs together. Together we can introduce NFTs to sneaker culture worldwide and create a foundation that can lead to integrationof real world fashion as a whole.

ROADMAP

The NFYE NFT Collection is a digitally verifiable, tradable, and fixed supply digital assets created as an exclusive collaboration and is the intersection of the Sneakerhead and NFT Community.

The NFYE community are inclusive, however NFYe Holders will have exclusive access to the following perks within the NFYe Community:

- Monthly Yeezy Community Giveaways
- Yeezy Digital Drops: NFTs of hyped Yeezy pairs, airdropped to NFYE holders wallets
- Limited Edition Yeezy Mafia and NFYE Merch Drops
- Brand Collaborations, both Merch and NFT Drops
- Artist Collaborations, both Merch and NFT Drops
- AMAs with Artists working in Collaboration with the NFYe Community

Our Goal is to give NFYe Holders Community both physical and digital rewards, and create an ecosystem that has real utility and rich incentives.
