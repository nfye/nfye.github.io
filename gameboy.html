ABOUT

For centuries, chimps dominated the jungle, thriving in the lush green tropical rainforest. Seeds, fruits, honey, and insects were in abundance, and there was little to nothing to worry about. Eat, sleep, and have some fun. This was the routine.

However, things began to change when man found the jungle and discovered how to make fire. One tree soon turned to two, three, and now the once green jungle is almost a desert.

Spartacus who was a simple chimp is now forced to scavenge for food and hide from humans. He grew tired of watching people cut their jungles, build fires, and steal their bananas.
